Communities are the backbone of any decentralized space but building a strong long-lasting one is not an easy task.

CanuDao is a diverse (this might be a community goal) and inclusive community made up of people of different ages, countries and backgrounds who share the same goal: we strive to help communities thrive and we do this by focusing on improving each member's experience.

Okay, but what problems do we solve?

One of the main challenges that all communities face is meeting the expectations of their members, and this is something difficult to achieve because there are some problems that are common in most communities:
  • A confusing onboarding process
  • No transparency or clear rewards
  • A lack of strategic planning and focus
  • Uninspired discussions
  • Low engagement
  • No pathways to build relationships amongst members

Treasury

Transparency first. Our treasury is managed fully on-chain trough the JuiceBox Protocol. You can go to juicebox.money/#/p/canudaocomm and see our payroll, balance, $CND token holders or maybe you want to get tokens yourself.

All payments are made trough JuiceBox and get tokens in return, so all clients are token holders as much as anyone who buys token directly in JuiceBox. The ETH/CND proportion goes down with time, so early contributors get better rewards.

Tokens will give you voting power in treasury related topics starting Q2 2022.
