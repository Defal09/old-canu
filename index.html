WE STRIVE TO HELP WEB3 COMMUNITIES THRIVE

The era of decentralized-autonomous WE.

Nothing is more heartbreaking than watching how what used to be a vibrant and lively community starts to fade away. Members get less involved overtime. Conversations become less and less stimulating. Relationships weaken. The value that was once co-created slowly dies away.

Members can't find incentives anymore... and the community becomes a graveyard.

RIP.

Building a strong long-lasting community is not an easy task, and in the case of DAOs the task gets even more demanding.

But we know that:

Communities are the backbone of any DAO.

And we also know that:

DAO's will be the future of communities in five years.

And here comes CanuDAO.

CanuDao is a diverse and inclusive community made up of people of different ages, countries and backgrounds who share the same goal: we strive to help communities thrive and we do this by focusing on improving each member's experience and creating a talent pipeline for community managers.

The problems we solve

One of the main challenges that all communities face is meeting the expectations of their members, which is something difficult to achieve of the common problems that are found in most communities: no focus on member's experience, confusing on boarding processes, no alignment of incentives, no transparency or clear rewards, no pathways to build relationships...

We combine our human talent and technological tools to provide solutions to these problems by:

1 Designing a meaningful community journey and experience for each member.

2 Creating a space where community builders can learn, grow, connect and find opportunities.
